About Liz

Made with intention,
rooted in community.

Hi, I'm Liz — and I've been beading for as long as I can remember. I grew up surrounded by the mountains, rivers, and forests of the Skeena Valley, and that landscape has never left my hands. Every piece I make carries a little bit of Terrace in it.

Elements by Liz started as a creative outlet and turned into something bigger when I realized how much people connected with handmade, locally-rooted work. I sell at local markets, work with community organizations, and do custom pieces for people right here in the northwest — and now shipping across Canada.

I believe in slow making, in supporting local, and in jewellery that actually means something. If you're looking for something personal, reach out — I'd love to create something just for you.
